Where are you now?

To utilize all Dinero has to offer you will manually input your monetary resources, responsibilities, and overall spending habits in real time. Through that you will see a snapshot of where your available funds are and how you got there each month.

Where are you going?

You want financial stability, sure. But you also want to spend money on things you want without the guilt. Dinero assists you by focusing your attention to your current spending habits. So by seeing where you are you can see where you are going: Financial success!

How do you get there?

By continually feeding Dinero HONEST information in a timely manner you will begin to see exactly where every penny you spend goes. And through that you will discover habits to adjust, habits to keep, and best practices to build. Putting a spotlight on your daily life will highlight some difficult truths about you and your spending habits.
